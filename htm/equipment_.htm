Equipment Guide

Rotating Manipulator Body

The Rotating Manipulator body houses the motor and electronics. The actuator drives the opening, closing and rotation of the attached jaw.

Rotating Manipulator Body

Rotating Manipulator Jaws

The five interchangeable jaws offer different capabilities of operation for the Rotating Manipulator.

Rotating Manipulator Jaws

Rotating Manipulator Skid

The Rotating Manipulator Skid is used to attach the Rotating Manipulator to the Pro 4 ROV.

Skid Plate

VideoRay Center Float

The VideoRay Center Float is used to provide buoyancy for the Rotating Manipulator when it is attached to the ROV without a BlueView sonar.

VideoRay Center Float

Counter Balance Weight

The Counter Balance Weight is used to provide stability to the ROV in water. The Counter Balance Weight remains below the water level to counteract the effects of weight on the ROV.

Counter Balance Weight

The Counterbalance Weight should not be used when the Rotating Manipulator is being used with a BlueView sonar. When the Rotating Manipulator and BlueView sonar are used together, there is no flotation capacity for any other accessories.

Blueview Center Float

The BlueView Center Float is used to provide buoyancy for the Rotating Manipulator when it is attached to the ROV with a BlueView sonar.

BlueView Center Float

Additional Equipment

Additional equipment provided with the Rotating Manipulator includes mounting screws and tool kit, and software.
